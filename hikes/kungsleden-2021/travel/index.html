Train, Plane, Hotel and Plane again

Getting to the start of the Kungsleden is quite a journey, at least when you live in switzerland or Rheinfelden to be more specific. There are various options, but we decided to go by plane in the end. As there are no direct (or even combined) flights available, we had to split the journey. So the first flight was Zurich -> Stockholm and as there was no flight which allowed us to capture the next flight to Hemavan on the same day, we went for a Hotel in Stockholm. We stayed in the Comfort Hotel Arlanda Airport, which we definitely can recommend (1090 SEK for two people). This was on the 20th of June, today (21st :)) we now left to take another plane from Stockholm to Hemavan (with a stop in Kramfors).
