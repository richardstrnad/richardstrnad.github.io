Good morning

We slept good and got up around 7 am, it was warm more or less the whole night. But in the morning the mosquitoes were still attached to our tent, so putting it down was bit of a challenge.

Way down

We hit the trail shortly before 8 am. After around 400m we saw Anton and Fredrik which were packing up. The last 6 km down to the boat were all through forest, so nothing too special.

Sometimes it opened up a bit and then there were nice views of the mountains.

Then we already reached the boat pier which provided an amazing view of the mountains in the back.

We were a bit early and arrive at around 09:30 am so we had to wait about an hour. This time was used for a revitalizing swim in the ice cold lake.
